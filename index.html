OVERVIEW

The Aqua Agriculture Market website is a platform designed to connect farmers and buyers in the aquaculture industry. It features sections for buying and selling aquatic products like fish, crabs, and prawns. Users can list their products for sale through a dedicated form and view available products in a marketplace section. The site includes a streamlined navigation menu for easy access to different pages, including home, product listings, and sales. Key functionalities include product management, purchase redirection, and payment processing. The design is user-friendly, with a compact footer and vibrant styling to enhance the overall user experience.
